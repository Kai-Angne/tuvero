scripts/backend/result.js

A Result represents the outcome of a game with two teams, usually including one to three players.

@param team1 Array of player ids of the first team. Creates an internal copy. @param team2 Array of player ids of the second team. Creates an internal copy. @param points1 {Integer} Points of the first team @param points2 {Integer} Points of the second team
